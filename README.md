# Digital-Literacy-Awareness
Digital literacy awareness means understanding how to use the internet, digital devices, and online platforms safely, responsibly, and effectively. It helps students protect their data, avoid cyber threats, and become responsible digital citizens.

**Project Overview**
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project submitted as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The aim of this project is to develop awareness about digital literacy, professional online presence, coding platforms, email etiquette, and cybercrime prevention.

The project consists of five tasks that demonstrate essential digital skills required by students in the modern digital world.
**Project Tasks**
**Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic**
In this task, I created an infographic explaining the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. The infographic highlights safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and maintaining a professional online presence.

Folder: task-1-presentation

**Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio**
In this task, I created professional profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help students showcase their skills, collaborate on projects, and build a professional network.

Folder: task-2-portfolio

**Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
In this task, I explored coding practice platforms by solving a beginner-level challenge on HackerRank. I also created a Google Form quiz to spread digital literacy awareness among students.

Folder: task-3-platforms

**Task 4 – Professional Email & Social Media Etiquette**
In this task, I wrote two professional emails:
